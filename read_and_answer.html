Read the following text and answer the comprehension questions.

My Job as an Actress

My name is Brenda. I am an actress. I always have a busy schedule. At the moment I am working on a new tv sitcom. It is quite difficult. I usually get up at 8 am but now I am getting up at 5 am because we start shooting at 7 am. The location isn´t in Los Angeles but in a small town in the desert. It is really hot. I like hot weather but not this hot.


It is hard work for 6 months the other 6 months I am with my family. My husband likes being married to an actress. He is a movie producer so sometimes we work together. At the moment we aren´t working together. He is at home with our children. I usually travel on the weekends and holidays to be with them. But this weekend I can´t go because we are shooting a special scene so they are giving us Monday and Tuesday so we can visit our families. I love working on TV but I miss my family.
